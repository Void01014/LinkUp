# LinkUp

LinkUp is a user-friendly social platform where people can easily connect, share, and communicate. 

## Key Features

- **Posts & Media**: Easily post text and images to share with your network.
- **Friend Circles**: Form friend circles by sending and accepting invites.
- **Direct Messaging (DMs)**: Text and send images directly to other users.
- **Live Status**: See who is online or offline with a live status indicator bubble.
- **Read Receipts**: Real-time feature to know if the messages you sent have been seen.
- **Disappearing Messages**: Automatically delete messages after 24 hours for enhanced privacy.
